Other Fantastic Cereals






























After fruity pebbles being my longtime favorite cereal, in a youtube video I stumbled upon what is now in my opinion one of the greatest cereals of all time, fruity pebbles with marshmallows. This cereal combines the perfect fruity taste with the delicious marshmallows you would typically find in lucky charms. If you haven’t tried them, I would highly recommend it.































Multigrain cheerios have been my go to cereal for a long time now. Not my favorite, but that reliable cereal that isn’t too sweet and is great for breakfast at any time. Throw in some fruit or just eat it plain, it’s great either way.
