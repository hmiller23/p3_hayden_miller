Here are some of my favorite athletes:

Frenkie has been my favorite soccer player for a number of years now. I love the energy he plays with as well as his overall play style. Over the past number of months I have really analyzed the way he has played and hopefully I have the opportunity to implement his play style into my game.
Nick Kyrgios is by far my favorite tennis player on the planet because of his unorthodox play style. There is never a boring moment when Nick is on the court. I have 100% mimicked my play style around him and attempt to do all of his cool tricks on court.
Aaron Donald is not only my favorite player in the NFL but in my opinion, he is also the best player in the entire league. He is so fun to watch and is an absolutely incredible athlete. I love being a rams fan but he makes it even more enjoyable.
